Tom's Story

I recently moved to the Raleigh-Durham area to pursue a career change because of the opportunities the area has to offer in the field of Web Development. While I may be new to the professional industry I have a long history of using the analytical way of thinking that development requires in my personal life. I have always enjoyed working with technology and began coding when I got my first Lego Mindstorm at the age of eight. That passion has stuck with me ever since. In high school I was on a robotics team that won regional - and competed in national - competitions for all four years and was the captain for the last three. I'm an avid reader, mostly of fiction, although I have started to transition to reading primarily nonfiction books. Most recently I've been reading American history (click here to check out my Goodreads profile to see what I'm currently reading). Aside from reading I'm also a big fan of camping, tennis, and skiing. If my experience and background interests you please contact me here, on my LinkedIn, or via e-mail! I'm open to remote and in office opportunities.
