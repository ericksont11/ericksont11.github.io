Tom's Story

I was born and raised in the Midwest and recently migrated to the Raleigh area to pursue the
opportunities the region has to offer. From being the captain of a robotics team for three years in high school, to running a
TEDx event and studying Biology at Xavier University in Cincinnati I've always had a passion for solving challenging problems. But the biggest
thing I feel I bring to any organization I'm apart of is my ability to amplify the skills of others. Though I pride myself on the techincal
knowledge that I've acquired everyone I've ever met has had all kinds of knowledge and skills that I don't. Because of this every group
I'm apart of is another chance to teach as well as learn new skills. Tech is the perfect field for me because it changes
so quickly and requires constant learning and development. Exactly what I love.
